# Why We Built Lumicade Solutions

Lumicade Solutions started with a simple observation: most software agencies only build for clients, and most SaaS companies only build their own products. We wanted to do both.

## The Dual Focus

By maintaining our own suite of SaaS microservices alongside client projects, we stay sharp on both sides of the product lifecycle. Building our own tools keeps us honest about developer experience, uptime, and scalability — lessons we bring directly to client work.

## What This Means for Clients

When you work with Lumicade, you're working with a team that ships production software every day — not just for you, but for our own users too. That means we understand the full lifecycle: from the first wireframe to post-launch monitoring.

## Based in Malaysia, Built for the World

We're proud to be a Malaysia-based team serving clients across Southeast Asia and beyond. Our timezone advantage means we're online when many teams aren't, and our rates are competitive without compromising on quality.
