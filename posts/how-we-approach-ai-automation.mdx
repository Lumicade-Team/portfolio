# How We Approach AI Automation

AI automation isn't about replacing people — it's about freeing them from repetitive work so they can focus on what matters. Here's how we approach it at Lumicade.

## Step 1: Identify the Bottleneck

Before writing a single line of code, we map out the workflow. Where are humans doing the same task over and over? Where do errors creep in? Where does a 2-minute task happen 200 times a day?

## Step 2: Choose the Right Tool

Not every problem needs a large language model. Sometimes a well-designed rule engine or a simple classification model is the right answer. We match the solution to the problem — not the other way around.

## Step 3: Build, Test, Deploy

We build automation pipelines in small, testable increments. Each step is validated against real data before moving to the next. By the time we deploy, the pipeline has already proven itself.

## Step 4: Monitor and Iterate

Automation isn't set-and-forget. We build in monitoring, alerting, and feedback loops so the system gets better over time — and so you know exactly what it's doing.
